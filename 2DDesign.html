2D Design

One of many sketches
"Visualising ideas into a picture that can share your ideas without the usage of words."
2D design was all about learning how to link everyday objects to deeper ideas and thoughts. I've learned to hide a more deeper meaning inside a work of mine through this module. Like how I depicted AI here; an always moving sliding puzzle, waiting to be completed so it can copy the face of humanity. Learning to do something like this gives a much more interesting view to how you can create something and how to send a message through art/design. I also got a much better understanding of Photoshop and Illustrator through this module, which is always handy!
