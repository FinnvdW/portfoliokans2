Sensors & Senses

The goal for Sensors & Senses was to create a mask that could help physically impaired people, based on 2 out of 5 possible senses, enjoy a festival. My chosen senses were sight and touch, with the idea that i could help people who have hearing problems. I learned how to code lights and vibrations timed with the sounds from a festival. The lights would be color coded on the loudness of the music, and the vibrations would be coded the same way.
"Changing your perception through technology,
while simultaneously changing the experience and interaction of your surroundings."
