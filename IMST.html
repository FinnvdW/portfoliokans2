Immersive Storytelling

For this module, we decided to focus on the subject of food. The story follows a butcher that is losing customers because of the rise in veganism. I was in charge of coding and decorating the game with the given props during this project, while also creating some props of my own. This taught me a lot about coding in general as well as 3D designing objects and animating those objects.
"Using storytelling and research to build a futuristic game in commission of PBL Netherlands Environmental Assessment Agency,
to make people think about the future and what is in store."
