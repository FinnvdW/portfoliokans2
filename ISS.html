Integrated Smart

Systems

"Combining smart objects, data and infrastructures to design a solution to a problem, or to improve the situation."
Together with a group, we created a system that allowed people to see how crowded shops and clubs were through an app. This app worked together with a live counter that counted people going in and out of places, as well as a timer that counted the amount of time you were inside. This time counter kept track for you in the app, so you could see what your favorite spaces were. I was in charge of coding and building that counter. In this module I learned how to create a live server that can track certain things live for you.
